I am a PhD candidate at RMIT University, advised by A/Prof. Andy Song and Prof. Xiaojun Chang. Before that, I was a research intern at Auto-Drive Lab, Alibaba DAMO Academy, where I was fortunate to work with Dr. Gang Wang and Dr. Bing Wang.

I received both my Master's degree and Bachelor's degree from South China University of Technology(SCUT).

Research interest

My research interests lie in computer vision and machine learning. Currently, I am working on efficient training and inference of tiny neural networks for edge computing. Previously, I was working on knowledge distillation to facilitate the compact networks. During my Master's thesis, I worked on semi-supervised learning and Generative Adversarial Networks.
