I am a PhD student at University of Technology Sydney, advised by Prof. Xiaojun Chang. Before that, I was a visiting student at MMlab, SIAT, Chinese Academy of Sciences, where I was fortunate to work with Prof. Yu Qiao/a>, and A/Prof. Yali Wang.

I received my Master's degress from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS) and my Bachelor's degree from Nankai University (NKU).

Research interest

My research interests lie in computer vision and machine learning. Currently, I am working on object and event prediction with NLP guidance in both videos and images, like Referring-VOS, video grounding and text-driven zero-shot object detection. Previously, I was working on individual and group activity recognition, and video object detection with full supervision and limited supervision. Previsouly, I worked on moving object detection and tracking during my Master's thesis.
